About Our Company

Mitee, a Sanskrit word derived from “ Bhumitee ” meaning Dimension. At Mitee we give you a vivid perception on designs, which helps you to tap into a totally new dimension. Hence our approach is to showcase your needs in a aesthetic yet functional form.

Sanjali Abhay Naik, Entrepreneur at Mitee Interiors runs the studio with her team of creative minds & contractors to maintain the quality which is expected by Client.

Our object here is to give at most satisfaction to client & creating a space which is designed in unique & logical way.
